Our Story

Mackay's Local Botanical Cafe since 1989

Lagoons Cafe is the place to plan your Gardens visit. Come relax or take in the view and local wildlife from the shady alfresco deck. Enjoy monthly exhibitions with an environmental or cultural theme in the air-conditioned Lagoons Gallery or plan a gardens escape. Lagoons Cafe can also cater for your next function or meeting at the Botanic Gardens - from casual gatherings and celebrations to events and wedding receptions.
